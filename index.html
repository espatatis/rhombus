About Us

Rhombus can help your child develop and grow in their academic pursuits by taking an assessment test at student orientation. During the orientation, your child will be tested on a variety of concepts from their grade level and beyond. Depending on their assessment Rhombus will begin with a progressive pathway that will help your child develop each skill to expert levels, enabling them to pursue academic success, helping them earn their best grades. Rhombus empowers students by building their confidence as your child masters skills useful not only in school but in real-world applications, reducing homework and test anxiety, as well as providing valuable feedback to give the student a sense of their growth.
